About Luz D´Habana



Luz D´Habana is a project integrated by musicians graduated from The Higher Institute of Arts and the National School of Arts, who have joined for fushing Cuban music with bebop, blues ,soul,smooth,jazz, latin, jazz, pop, bossa nova, reggae, funk , etc.



The project aims to rescue the oldest forms of each gender and interpret them using current execution modes. In addition, it is mixed with the international music for their relevance and originality has transcended fron previous years and is a paradigm of music worldwide. Thus, and entertaining pace will be achieved, with a duality of convergence between old and new jazz, classical music, while interacting with Cuban music genres.



The composition of topics has been made that covers the full range of the genre of Latin jazz, mixing with Brazilian rhythms, Afro-Cuban, flamenco and the varied roots of Cuban music such as son, nengón,cha cha, danzon, bolero among others. The project has participated in the last two editions of the Jazz Plaza Festival in Havana.



In Cuban television programs like “De tarde en Casa”, “Entre manos”. In Radio programs in relevant stations in the country as “Radio Taino”, “Radio Havana Cuba”, “Radio Guama”.
